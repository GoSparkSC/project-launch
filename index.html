Project Launch
We connect inner-city high school students to relevant college-student mentors, and help our students build the hard skills, soft skills, and growth mindset they will need as leaders of the next generation.
The Program
MENTORS
We connect USC students to inner-city high school students with shared interests. Mentors meet with mentees at least twice a month, and helps facillitate trips to tech and entrepreneurship hubs in Los Angeles.
